Welcome to The Church of Pentecost - Salem Assembly

A Spirit-filled community where we experience the power and presence of God. We are glad to have you here. Join us in our journey of faith and community. We'd love to welcome you in person soon!

Vision & Mission Statement of the church

Vision Statement

To become a Church that impacts its world with the values and principles of the Kingdom of God.

Mission Statement

To transform every community with the glorious Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Tenets of the church

The Bible
The One True God
Man's Depraved Nature
The Savior
Repentance, Justification, and Sanctification
The Ordinances of Baptism and the Lord's Supper
Baptism, Gifts, and Fruit of the Holy Spirit
Divine Healing
Tithes and Offerings
The Second Coming and the Next Life

Our Ministries

these are the ministries in our local assembly

Women's Ministry

Equipping women to live godly lives, support their families, and impact others with love and service.

Men's Ministry

Empowering men to grow spiritually, lead in their families, and serve in the church and community.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to develop their faith, build relationships, and make a difference in their communities.

Outreach Ministry

Equipping individuals to share their faith, reach out to the community, and make disciples.
